My favourite places in Glasgow

One of the things I miss the most is to go out and try new restaurants and cafe. So I thought the next closest thing would be to have a virtual trip, while waiting to be able to go out again. Since we're all stuck inside, I decided to collect all the memories I have from my favourite restaurants and cafes in Glagsow and put them in this blog.

Pot Belly

I have been wanting to go to this cafe for ages. One friday morning I decided to treat myself and have a trip to the southside to try it out. What a lush breakfast! That's excatly where I will go for brunch as soon as they open again. It is the cutest little cafe and the staff is even sweeter than the pancakes! I also peacked into the kitchen and I'm desperate to work in such a lovely and fun environment.

Gnom

I went to this cafe on a sunny saturday morning, together with my sister who came to visit me from Italy. The place was full of people all happily enjoying their brunch: the perfect atmosphere. The service was really amazing and I wanted to try every single thing on the menu. All the dishes had different twist, unxpected flavours and combination. I am really looking forward to be back and try something new.

Alchemilla

I am not sure if I can find the words to describe how amazing the food was in this restaurant. Everything was so beatiful, balanced, delicate, but also surprising, new, unexpected. The restaurant is absolutely gorgeous, I love the warm colours of the walls and the style of tables and chairs. also it was lovely to being able to see the chefs while they were cooking. I am so looking forward to be back.

Curler's Rest

The place I am more looking forward to be back to is just a regular pub with simple food and a homely atmosphere. The food is tasty and comforting, the sofas are cosy, the lights are low. This is the place where I went on the first date with the most amazing guy I have ever met and who is now my boyfriend. I am looking forward to be back with him, have a pint and some fish and chips. Everything will be all right again.

I hope you enjoyed this virtual trip to some of my favourite food spots in Glasgow.
